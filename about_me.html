About Me
I am currently an Associate Control Systems Engineer at Contech Control Services, where I work on the design, implementation, and maintenance of control systems for various customers. I graduated from Texas A&M University in May 2024 with a Bachelors in Computer Science, with an emphasis in robotics and high-performance computing. I love to learn, and I am always looking for new opportunities to grow and develop my skills. Feel free to contact me at CRNicholls20@gmail.com, or through my LinkedIn page.
Outside of work, I enjoy reading, rock climbing, cooking and baking, chess, card games, and TTRPGs. I am currently running a D&D campaign for my friends, and I am working on a novel in my free time.
